## Welcome to Polygon Animals

Polygon Animals is a React-based interactive web application that brings awareness to 10 endangered marine species in Thai waters. Using low-poly geometric art and immersive parallax scrolling, it creates a visually stunning educational experience about ocean conservation.

## The visual metaphor

Each animal is rendered as hundreds of colored polygon shards. When assembled, they form a recognizable silhouette — a visual metaphor for ecosystem fragility.

> "If any single piece is missing, the whole image falls apart — just as the loss of any species disrupts the entire ecosystem."
